Program Intent

Build the engine before the arena asks for it

Gladiator Cardio replaces passive steady-state work with purposeful conditioning. Sled drags, assault-bike intervals, loaded circuits, and benchmark rounds are used to improve work capacity, speed of recovery, and your ability to keep making good decisions when fatigue starts to take over.
