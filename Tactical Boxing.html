Program Intent

The sweet science, stripped down to what matters

Tactical Boxing focuses on the mechanics that actually move the needle: stance discipline, ring movement, defensive responsibility, punch selection, and round management. Every session is designed to sharpen your technique while building the conditioning needed to keep that technique intact when the pace rises.

Weekly Regiment

Four touchpoints with clear tactical intent

Monday Technique and partner drills Stance, straight-line mechanics, and clean defensive responses.
Wednesday Heavy bag and power output Sharp combinations, round pacing, and force transfer through structure.
